Our Story

Every great journey starts with a single step — and ours began in 2015 with a humble Indica Vista, driven by the vision of our founder, Mr. Swapnil Adhao (CEO). What started as a small dream soon grew into a full-fledged travel company, built on trust, commitment and customer satisfaction.

By 2016, Trip Triangles Tours & Travels was formally registered, ensuring complete compliance with government norms. The following year 2017, we proudly became a GST-registered travel company, a mark of professionalism and growth.

Today, we are one of Pune’s reliable travel service providers, offering a wide fleet of vehicles – from comfortable sedans and SUVs to travellersmini-buses, and luxury buses – for every type of journey. Whether it’s a local city ride, an outstation family trip, a corporate booking, wedding travel, or a group tour, we have the perfect travel solution.

  • Reliable cab rental services
  • Professional driver-on-rent solutions
  • Bus & corporate bookings
  • Daily Pune–Mumbai cab service
  • Group, family, wedding & outstation trips
