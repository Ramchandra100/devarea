Our Story

Trip Triangles Tours and Travels began its journey in 2015 with just a small hatchback cab (Indica Vista), driven by the passion and vision of our founder Mr. Swapnil Ashok Adhao – a Computer Engineer from Pune University.

In 2016, we were officially registered as a company, and by 2017, we became a GST-registered firm—building credibility and trust among our customers.

From day one, our mission has been simple: to make every journey safe, comfortable, and memorable. With hard work, honesty, and the love of our loyal customers, we’ve grown from one car to a wide fleet serving every travel need—big or small.

  • Reliable cab rental services
  • Professional driver-on-rent solutions
  • Bus & corporate bookings
  • Daily Pune–Mumbai cab service
  • Group, family, wedding & outstation trips
