Our Mission

From the very beginning, our mission has been simple: to make every journey safe, comfortable, and memorable. Over the years, with hard work, honesty, and the support of our loyal customers, we have grown from one car to a wide fleet of sedans, SUVs, travellers, mini-buses, and buses – serving every travel need, big or small.

100% Safety 98% Satisfaction

Our Story

Trip Triangles Tours and Travels began its journey in 2015 with just a small Hatchback cab- Indica Vista, driven by the passion and vision of our founder Mr. Swapnil Adhao – a Computer Engineer from Pune University. What started as a single-car service with a big dream soon turned into a trusted travel company. In 2016, Trip Triangles was officially registered as a company, and by 2017, we became GST registered – building credibility and trust among our customers.

Swapnil Adhao, Founder
