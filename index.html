Our Story

Every great journey starts with a single step — and ours began in 2015 with a humble Indica Vista, driven by the vision of our founder, Mr. Swapnil Adhao. What started as a small dream soon grew into a full-fledged travel company, built on trust, commitment, and customer satisfaction.

By 2016, Trip Triangles Tours & Travels was formally registered, ensuring complete compliance with government norms. The following year 2017, we proudly became a GST-registered travel company, a mark of professionalism and growth.

Swapnil Adhao
(CEO & Founder)
